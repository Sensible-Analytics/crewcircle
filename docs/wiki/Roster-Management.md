# Roster Management

Learn how to create and manage employee rosters in CrewCircle.

## Creating Shifts

### Adding a Single Shift

1. Go to the **Roster** page
2. Click on an empty cell in the roster grid
3. Enter the shift details:
   - Start time
   - End time
   - Assigned employee
   - Location (if multiple locations)
4. Click **Save**

### Copying from Previous Week

1. Select the week you want to copy from
2. Click **Copy Week**
3. Select the destination week
4. Confirm the copy

This is useful for maintaining consistent rosters.

## Conflict Detection

CrewCircle automatically detects scheduling conflicts:

| Conflict Type | Description | Warning |
|--------------|-------------|---------|
| Double Booking | Employee scheduled for overlapping shifts | Red highlight |
| Break Violation | Less than minimum break between shifts | Orange highlight |
| Availability | Shift conflicts with employee's availability | Yellow highlight |
| Maximum Hours | Shift exceeds weekly hour limits | Blue highlight |

## Publishing Rosters

When you're ready to share the roster with your team:

1. Review all shifts
2. Click **Publish Roster**
3. Confirm the publish action

Employees will receive:
- Push notification on their mobile app
- Email notification (if enabled)

## Editing Published Rosters

You can still edit a published roster:

1. Click **Unpublish** to make changes
2. Make your edits
3. Click **Publish Roster** again

Changes will be notified to affected employees.

## Multi-Location Support

If your business has multiple locations:

1. Add locations in **Settings > Locations**
2. Assign locations to shifts
3. Filter roster view by location

## Roster Views

### Week View (Default)
- Shows 7 days at a time
- Best for planning weekly schedules

### Day View
- Shows single day with more detail
- Best for reviewing specific shifts

## Exporting Rosters

To export roster data:

1. Go to **Roster**
2. Click **Export**
3. Choose date range
4. Select format (CSV)
5. Download the file

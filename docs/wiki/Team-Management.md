# Team Management

Manage your employees, roles, and permissions in CrewCircle.

## Roles & Permissions

### Owner
- Full access to all features
- Can manage billing and subscriptions
- Can delete the business account
- Cannot be removed

### Manager
- Can create and publish rosters
- Can approve timesheets
- Can invite employees (with owner approval)
- Cannot manage billing

### Employee
- Can view their roster
- Can clock in/out
- Can update their availability
- Cannot view other employees' data

## Inviting Employees

### Sending Invitations

1. Go to **Settings > Team**
2. Click **Invite Employee**
3. Enter their email address
4. Select their role
5. Click **Send Invitation**

The employee will receive an email with a link to join your business.

### Accepting Invitations

Employees can:

1. Click the link in their email
2. Create their account (or log in)
3. They'll automatically be added to your business

## Managing Availability

Employees can set their availability:

1. Go to **My Profile > Availability**
2. Set regular weekly availability
3. Add one-off exceptions
4. Submit for manager review

Managers can view and approve availability in **Team > Availability**.

## Employee Profiles

### Required Information

- Full name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Employment classification (award)
- Tax file number (stored securely)

### Optional Information

- Profile photo
- Emergency contact
- Qualifications
- Notes

## Removing Employees

### Soft Delete

When you remove an employee:

1. Their timesheet history is preserved
2. Their profile is marked as inactive
3. They lose access to the business

To remove an employee:

1. Go to **Settings > Team**
2. Find the employee
3. Click **Remove**
4. Confirm the action

### Reactivating

Previously removed employees can be re-invited using the same process as new invitations.

## Profile Photos

Employees can upload profile photos:

1. Go to **My Profile**
2. Click on the current photo
3. Upload a new image
4. Crop to fit
5. Save

Profile photos appear in:
- Roster view
- Time clock
- Timesheet reports
- Notifications

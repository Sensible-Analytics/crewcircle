# Australian Compliance

CrewCircle is built with Australian workplace compliance at its core.

## Fair Work Act Compliance

### Record Keeping Requirements

Under the Fair Work Act, employers must keep records of:

- Hours worked by each employee
- Start and end times of work
- Breaks taken
- Overtime hours
- Leave entitlements

CrewCircle automatically maintains these records for **7 years**, meeting Fair Work Act requirements.

### Maximum Hours

CrewCircle warns when:

- Full-time employees exceed 38 hours/week
- Shifts exceed 10 hours
- Minimum breaks between shifts are violated

### Break Requirements

The system tracks:

- Meal breaks (30-60 minutes unpaid)
- Rest breaks (10 minutes paid)
- Cumulative fatigue breaks

## Modern Award Support

### Award Detection

When you add employees, CrewCircle:

1. Asks for their employment classification
2. Identifies applicable modern award
3. Applies award-specific rules

### Supported Awards

- General Retail Industry Award
- Hospitality Industry Award
- Fast Food Industry Award
- Medical Services Award
- And many more...

## Privacy Act Compliance

### Data Collection

We collect only what's necessary:

- Employee contact details
- Work location
- Time records
- Roster preferences

### Data Storage

All data is stored in:

- **Location**: AWS Sydney (ap-southeast-2)
- **Encryption**: At rest and in transit
- **Retention**: 7 years minimum

### Employee Rights

Employees can:

- Request access to their data
- Request correction of errors
- Withdraw consent for marketing

## ABN & Business Verification

### ABN Validation

All business accounts must provide a valid ABN. We validate using:

- ABR (Australian Business Register) algorithm
- Modulus 89 check digit validation
- Business name matching

### Employee Verification

Employees are verified by:

- Email confirmation
- ABN association (for business linking)

## Best Practices

1. **Keep records up to date**: Review and approve timesheets weekly
2. **Communicate changes**: Notify employees of roster changes promptly
3. **Document exceptions**: Note any variations from standard arrangements
4. **Review alerts**: Pay attention to CrewCircle compliance warnings

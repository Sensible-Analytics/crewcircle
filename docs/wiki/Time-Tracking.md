# Time Tracking

CrewCircle's GPS-verified time clock ensures accurate tracking of employee hours.

## How Clock In/Out Works

### For Employees

1. Open the CrewCircle mobile app
2. Navigate to **Time Clock**
3. Tap **Clock In**
4. The app will verify your location
5. If within geofence, clock in is recorded
6. To clock out, tap **Clock Out**

### Geofence Verification

The time clock uses GPS to verify employees are at their work location:

- **Within geofence**: Clock in/out recorded normally
- **Outside geofence**: Warning shown, clock in/out flagged
- **Location unavailable**: Clock in/out blocked until location enabled

## Timesheet Management

### Viewing Timesheets

1. Go to **Timesheets**
2. Select the date range
3. View all clock in/out entries

### Approving Timesheets

Managers and owners can approve timesheet entries:

1. Go to **Timesheets**
2. Review entries for the period
3. Click **Approve** for individual entries
4. Or click **Approve All** for batch approval

### Exporting Timesheets

For payroll processing:

1. Go to **Timesheets**
2. Select the date range
3. Click **Export CSV**
4. Open in Excel or import to payroll software

The CSV includes:
- Employee name
- Date
- Clock in time
- Clock out time
- Total hours
- Location
- Approval status

## Real-Time Tracking

Managers can see live clock status:

1. Go to **Team Status**
2. View who's currently clocked in
3. See their clock in time
4. View remaining shift hours

## Offline Support

The mobile app works offline:

- Employees can clock in/out without internet
- Data syncs when connection is restored
- Timestamp is recorded at device time

## Accuracy & Auditing

All time entries include:

- Device timestamp
- GPS coordinates
- Geofence status
- Manager approval (if required)

This creates a complete audit trail for Fair Work Act compliance.

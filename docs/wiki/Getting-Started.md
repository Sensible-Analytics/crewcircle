# Getting Started with CrewCircle

Welcome to CrewCircle! This guide will help you get up and running with our Australian workforce management platform.

## Prerequisites

Before you start, make sure you have:

- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- A valid Australian Business Number (ABN) for your business
- Employee email addresses for team members

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Visit [crewcircle.co/signup](https://crewcircle.co/signup)
2. Enter your work email address
3. Create a strong password (must include uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character)
4. Enter your business name
5. Enter your Australian Business Number (ABN)

Your ABN will be validated using the official ABR algorithm.

## Step 2: Set Up Your Team

Once logged in, you can invite employees:

1. Navigate to **Settings > Team**
2. Click **Invite Employee**
3. Enter their email address
4. Select their role:
   - **Manager**: Can edit rosters and approve timesheets
   - **Employee**: Can view rosters and clock in/out

## Step 3: Add Work Locations

If your business operates from multiple locations:

1. Go to **Settings > Locations**
2. Click **Add Location**
3. Enter the address
4. Set the geofence radius (recommended: 100-200 meters)

Employees will only be able to clock in when they're within the geofence.

## Step 4: Create Your First Roster

1. Go to **Roster**
2. Select the week you want to create
3. Click on a cell to add a shift
4. Drag the shift to adjust times
5. Assign employees to shifts
6. Click **Publish Roster** when ready

Your team will receive push notifications when the roster is published.

## Step 5: Set Up Billing (Optional)

For the free tier, no billing setup is required.

To upgrade to Starter:

1. Go to **Settings > Billing**
2. Click **Upgrade Plan**
3. Enter your payment details
4. Confirm the upgrade

You'll be charged $4 + GST per active employee per month.

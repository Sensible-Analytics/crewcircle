## Revenue Models That Work

### Freemium → Paid Conversion (Most Common)
- Free tier with scan limits (50-300 scans)
- Individual Pro: $3-15/month
- Team plans: $60-100/user/year
- Enterprise: Custom pricing

### B2B/Enterprise (Highest Revenue)
- ScanBizCards: $100/user/year for teams up to 50
- snapAddy: Per-user annual subscription (enterprise focus, DAX companies)
- iCapture: ~$500/event or subscription for trade shows
- Popl: $18.4M revenue primarily from B2B event lead capture

### Hardware + Software (NFC)
- Mobilo: NFC card hardware + software subscription
- Wave Connect: NFC cards + Pro subscription ($7/mo individual, $60/user/year teams)
- v1ce: NFC card hardware sales

## CRM Integration Demand (Most to Least Demanded)
1. **Salesforce** - Most enterprise demand, premium pricing justified
2. **HubSpot** - Has built-in free scanner, huge SMB market
3. **Zoho CRM** - Has built-in scanner, growing market
4. **Google Contacts** - Universal, free tier standard
5. **Outlook/Microsoft Dynamics** - Enterprise, B2B
6. **Pipedrive** - Sales-focused SMB
7. **SugarCRM** - Niche enterprise

## OCR/AI Technology Stack (2026)
- **Google Document AI**: $1.50-$30/1K pages, best for complex layouts
- **AWS Textract**: $0.0015/page, quick deployment
- **Azure Document Intelligence**: Strong enterprise, deprecated business card model in v4.0
- **ABBYY FineReader Engine**: Traditional OCR leader, enterprise
- **Tesseract.js**: Open source, client-side
- **Google Gemini + Tesseract combo**: Emerging pattern (99% accuracy claimed)
- **PaddleOCR 3.0**: Open source, strong multilingual
- **DeepSeek OCR**: New entrant 2025

**NOTE**: Azure deprecated its dedicated `prebuilt-businessCard` model in v4.0 - apps using it must migrate

## B2B vs B2C Analysis
- **B2B is more profitable**: Higher ARPU, lower churn, enterprise contracts
- B2B segment dominated in 2022 at 67% revenue share (Allied Market Research)
- Enterprise deals: $100-500+/user/year vs $3-15/month consumer
- B2B use cases: Trade shows, sales teams, CRM integration, lead capture
- B2C use cases: Individual networking, freelancers, job seekers
- **Winning strategy**: Freemium B2C funnel → B2B team/enterprise upsell

## Key Insights for Product Strategy
1. The "scanner app" category is converging with "digital business card platform"
2. CRM integration is table stakes for B2B - Salesforce + HubSpot are must-haves
3. Team features (shared contacts, admin dashboard, rep attribution) are the B2B differentiator
4. ABBYY deprecated cloud sync Sept 2023 - opportunity to capture their users
5. BizConnect likely deprecated - another capture opportunity
6. Azure deprecated dedicated business card model - forces migration
7. NFC hardware + software bundle creates hardware moat (Mobilo model)
8. Contact enrichment (LinkedIn pull) is emerging as key differentiator
9. Offline mode is important for trade show use cases
10. Asia Pacific is fastest growing market (India at 19.5% CAGR)
